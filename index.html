Compliance & Ethics

Procurement Support, Done Properly

Affiliate Warehouse operates as a procurement support and price-monitoring service — not a reseller, not a circumvention tool.

📋

Procurement Support Only

Affiliate Warehouse operates strictly as a procurement support and price-monitoring service. No goods are resold. No supplier terms are altered.

🔒

No Resale of Goods

All products are purchased directly through wholesaler channels for the contractor's own use. Affiliate Warehouse does not take possession of or resell any goods.

⚖️

Wholesaler Terms Unchanged

Pricing, availability, and terms are set solely by wholesalers. No supplier terms are modified, circumvented, or altered in any way.

🙋

Voluntary Participation

Participation is entirely voluntary and non-exclusive. Contractors are never obligated to purchase through Affiliate Warehouse — comparison is always encouraged.

🔭

Transparent Comparison

Contractors are explicitly encouraged to compare Affiliate Warehouse pricing against their existing trade accounts. Transparency is central to the model.

🏗️

Industry-Safe by Design

The model is designed from the ground up to benefit both contractors and the supply chain — improving efficiency and volume without disrupting wholesaler relationships.
